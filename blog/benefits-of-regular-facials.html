The Benefits of Regular Facials

By Falling Waters Day Spa | Updated February 2026 | 6 min read

You wash your face daily, maybe use a serum or moisturizer, but is that enough? While a good at-home routine is essential, professional facials offer benefits that products alone simply can't match.

At Falling Waters Day Spa in Draper, Utah, our licensed estheticians have seen firsthand how consistent facial treatments transform skin. Here's why making facials a regular part of your routine is one of the best investments you can make in your skin.

Why Monthly Facials Make a Difference

Your skin cells turn over approximately every 28-30 days. By getting a facial once a month, you're working with your skin's natural renewal cycle to:

  • Remove accumulated dead skin cells
  • Clear clogged pores before they become problems
  • Maintain consistent hydration levels
  • Address skin concerns before they worsen
  • Keep your skin in optimal health year-round

What Happens If You Skip Facials?

Without regular professional care, you may notice:

  • More frequent breakouts from clogged pores
  • Dull, uneven skin tone
  • Faster visible aging
  • Increased texture and roughness
  • Less effective results from your at-home products

How Often Should You Get a Facial?

  • General maintenance: Once a month
  • Acne-prone skin: Every 2-3 weeks during flare-ups
  • Anti-aging focus: Every 3-4 weeks
  • Before special events: 1-2 weeks prior
