Couples Massage Date Night Ideas

By Falling Waters Day Spa | Updated February 2026 | 5 min read

Looking for a date night that's more memorable than dinner and a movie? A couples massage offers something unique: quality time together in a peaceful, romantic setting where you can both fully relax and reconnect.

At Falling Waters Day Spa in Draper, Utah, we've hosted countless couples celebrating anniversaries, Valentine's Day, birthdays, or simply wanting to escape the daily grind together. Here are our favorite ideas for making your couples spa experience extra special.

Why Couples Massage Makes the Perfect Date

  • Shared experience: You're doing something together, not just sitting side by side
  • Stress relief: Both of you leave relaxed and in a better mood
  • No phones: Genuine, uninterrupted quality time
  • Memory-making: It's unique and memorable
  • Health benefits: Massage releases oxytocin, the "bonding hormone"

Before Your Appointment

  • Book in advance, especially for weekends and holidays
  • Communicate any preferences (pressure, focus areas) separately if needed
  • Avoid heavy meals right before your massage
  • Arrive 15 minutes early to start relaxing together

During Your Massage

  • It's okay to hold hands if the tables are close enough
