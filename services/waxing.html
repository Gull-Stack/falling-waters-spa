Waxing FAQ

What to know before your appointment

How long should hair be for waxing?

Hair should be about 1/4 inch (roughly 2-3 weeks of growth). Too short and the wax can't grip; too long and it may be more uncomfortable.

How long do results last?

Typically 3-6 weeks depending on your hair growth cycle. With regular waxing, hair often grows back finer and sparser over time.

Is waxing painful?

There's some discomfort, but our estheticians use techniques to minimize it. Most clients find it very manageable, especially after the first few visits.

What should I do before my appointment?

Gently exfoliate 24 hours before. Avoid sun exposure, retinol products, and caffeine on the day of your appointment.
