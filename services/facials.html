Facial FAQ

Common questions about our facial treatments

How often should I get a facial?

For best results, we recommend monthly facials. This aligns with your skin's natural cell turnover cycle and maintains consistent improvement.

What's the difference between HydraFacial and a regular facial?

HydraFacial uses patented vortex technology for deeper cleansing and hydration. It's more intensive than traditional facials with zero downtime.

Is there downtime after a chemical peel?

Light peels have minimal downtime (slight redness). Medium to deep peels may cause peeling for 3-7 days. We'll discuss what to expect.

Can I wear makeup after my facial?

We recommend waiting 24 hours to let products absorb and skin breathe. Your esthetician will provide specific aftercare instructions.
