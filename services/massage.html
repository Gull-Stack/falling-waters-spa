Massage FAQ

Common questions about our massage services

How do I know which massage is right for me?

Our therapists will discuss your goals and concerns before your session to recommend the best treatment. Swedish is great for relaxation, while deep tissue targets specific pain points.

What should I wear during my massage?

Undress to your comfort level. You'll be professionally draped throughout the session. We provide robes for your comfort before and after treatment.

How often should I get a massage?

For general wellness, once a month is beneficial. For chronic pain or high stress, every 2-3 weeks is recommended. Athletes may benefit from weekly sessions.

Is deep tissue massage painful?

You may experience some discomfort in tight areas, but it shouldn't be painful. Always communicate with your therapist about pressure preferences.

Can I get a massage if I'm pregnant?

Yes! Our prenatal massage is specifically designed for expecting mothers (second trimester onwards). It's safe and can help relieve pregnancy-related discomforts.

How early should I arrive?

Please arrive 10-15 minutes before your appointment to check in, change, and begin relaxing before your treatment starts.
